Macro: tcflag_t IEXTEN
POSIX.1 gives IEXTEN implementation-defined meaning,
so you cannot rely on this interpretation on all systems.

On BSD systems and GNU/Linux and GNU/Hurd systems, it enables the LNEXT and
DISCARD characters.
